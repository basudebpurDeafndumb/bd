RESIDENTIAL INSTITUTION

We started our work of welfare and development at Basudebpur, P.O. Pairachali, P.S. Falta, Dist. South 24 Parganas, in Falta Block, West Bengal with the facility of providing a Residential Primary School for the persons who are Deaf & Dumb. At present we have Classes from 1 to VIII.

Our Secretory

Parimal Mandal

(Secretory)

"Our dedicated and specially trained teachers play a pivotal role in running the Primary and Upper Primary School, focusing on the welfare and empowerment of our beneficiaries. Through diverse skill training and vocational courses, we aim to equip individuals with hearing and speech impairments with the tools to lead independent, confident, and enlightened lives. Our curriculum is designed to foster independence and transform them into economically productive citizens capable of shouldering responsibilities for both themselves and the community. By providing a supportive learning environment, we strive to enable the Deaf and Dumb community to not only overcome challenges but also contribute meaningfully to society "
